Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Which resort has the best actual valley view?

+
Both Zacs Valley and DVARA receive five-star ratings for view quality — guests at both properties describe unobstructed, panoramic valley panoramas from their rooms and balconies. At Sterling Kodai Valley, views are good but depend on your floor; always request an upper-floor room when booking.

Is Zacs Valley Resort worth the higher price?

+
For couples and wellness travellers, yes. The combination of private pool villas, a proper Ayurveda centre, exceptional staff, and TripAdvisor's 2025 Travellers' Choice Award make it the strongest overall offering. The main trade-off is the rough 6 km approach road — bring an SUV or crossover, not a sedan.

Can I reach these resorts without a private vehicle?

+
Most resorts can arrange airport or town transfers on request. However, being located on the outskirts of Kodaikanal, all three properties are difficult to reach by public transport. It's strongly recommended to either self-drive, hire a taxi for the duration of your stay, or pre-book resort transfers.

Which resort is best for a family with young children?

+
Sterling Kodai Valley is the strongest choice for families with young children — it has a structured in-house activity programme, supervised outdoor games, a kids' club, and large 2BR family suites that comfortably fit groups of 4–6. Zacs Valley also has a kids' club and play areas if you prefer a more upscale setting.

Is DVARA better than Sterling Kodai Valley?

+
For couples and small groups, DVARA scores higher in staff quality, food freshness, cleanliness, and valley view quality — often at a lower price point. However, DVARA has only 3 cottages and no pool or spa. Sterling wins for families needing scale, activities, and multiple room configurations.

When should I book to secure a room?

+
DVARA books up particularly fast given its boutique scale of just 3 cottages — book at least 4–6 weeks ahead for peak season (April–June and December–January). Zacs Valley and Sterling have more capacity, but valley-view villas at Zacs also fill up quickly. Book 2–4 weeks in advance for non-peak travel.
