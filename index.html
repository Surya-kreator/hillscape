Into the Silver Mist of Kodaikanal

There's a particular quality of morning light at 7,000 feet — the way it drifts through pine-scented mist, softening every ridge and valley into shades of silver and blue. Kodaikanal doesn't simply welcome you; it invites you to slow down, to wander its forested paths and quiet viewpoints, and to rediscover a gentler rhythm hidden high above the plains.
